INTERNSHIPS

Internships Provided by American Skills & Personality Development

ASPD (AMERICAN SKILLS & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT), is an organization devoted to promoting cross-cultural awareness through international internships, educations and cultural exchange programs. For more than 7 years, ASPD has been connecting young people from all over INDIA with life-changing international cultural exchange and internships opportunities.

We encourage youth to travel and see the world while they are young so they can develop a strong cultural foundation that there is far bigger world out there to enjoy and learn. Our Internships are focused on fields such as hotel/restaurant management, culinary arts & tourism management.

USA INTERSHIPS: 6-12 MONTHS

Front Office

This position offers participants the opportunity to understand the procedures and daily operation of the hotel guest service operations. Training focuses on service standards, complaint resolution, teamwork and learning the position in order to understand its management and overall role in the operation. Practical training assignments/ rotations may include front desk, reservations, concierge, PBX, retail, intro to housekeeping supervisor.

Food and Bevarage Service

This position offers participants the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the food service operation within the hospitality industry. Participants will be exposed to the daily operations within the food service industry, while performing their practical training assignments. Practical training assignments/rotations may include: server assistant, host/hostess, food service, room service, beverage service, banquet/catering service.

Culinary/Food Production

This position provides participants the opportunity to gain hands on culinary experience while being exposed to the operation of a culinary department within the hospitality industry. Practical training may include food preparation, sauces, stock, line cooking, pastry, breads, dessert, inventory, and kitchen sanitation.

UAE Hospitality Internships

Our Hospitality Internships in UAE was designed to provide participants with a practical working environment. Students get the opportunity to undertake paid work experience abroad and learning the culture and language. Participants on this internships are given paid jobs in the hospitality industry in UAE. The jobs range from working in Hotels, restaurants, kitchens, welcome desks and so forth – all within popular resorts and hotels in Spain.

Mauritus Internships

Our Mauritius Hospitality Internship Program offers candidates an opportunity to gain professional work experience and improve career opportunities in Hospitality,Toursm and Culinary industry. The Hospitality Program is a paid internship program offered to highly skilled candidates that are studying or have completed hospitality studies and have relevant hospitality experience. There are 6 & 12 months options. Positions are available in Food and Beverage, Front Office and Cookery.

France Internship

Our France Hospitality Internships provides fantastic opportunities to anyone looking for a paid work placement (optional) as well as the opportunity to improve their French language in a professional Hospitality environment. France has a huge shortfall of workers in the hospitality sector, especially in the more rural, naturally beautiful locations of France. Positions are available in Food and Beverage, Front Office and Culinary. We arrange for 3, 6 and 12 month Hospitality, Hotel Management and Culinary internships in France.
