Our Internship Programs

The ASPD internship program is designed to enable Hospitality college students to participate in hospitality-related practical training experiences as part of their academic training. The internship program allows students an opportunity to apply theory to real-world practicals, acquire career experience, and enhance personal growth while earning an International certificate from a reputed Hotel or Resort.

ASPD Internships have partnerships with International Hospitality programs that provide training ranging from 3 to 12 months all over the world. It is a unique academic program that combines classroom learning with practical work experience. Our Internship Placement Program provides students and young professionals the opportunity to gain professional experience and expand their horizons through on-the-job training with a reputed star Hotel or Resort in the USA.
